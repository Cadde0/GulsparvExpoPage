    • Our Product

    Sprout View is a web application that simplifies gardening by offering personalized care advice tailored to your environment. It helps you choose the right plants, provides customized care instructions, and predicts plant growth, making plant care straightforward and rewarding for gardeners at any level.

    About Us

    We are Team Gulsparv, a group of students majoring in Information and Communications Engineering at KTH. As plant enthusiasts, we understand the challenges many face in gardening, from choosing the right plants to ensuring they grow healthily. This inspired us to create a solution that bridges the gap between technology and gardening, making it accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Our team is united by a shared passion for technology, driving us to innovate and create tools that enhance the gardening experience. Each member of our team brings a unique skill set, from coding and design to research and development, all contributing to the success of SproutView.
